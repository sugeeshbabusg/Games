Instructions
1.Install Pydroid 3 on your smartphone.
2.Make sure pygame is installed on it.
3.Open the file speed_dash.py in the app.
4.Run the game.
5.Drag the player to move and avoid obstacles. 
5.If there is a lag in gameplay, it may be due to your screen resolution. Tweak the game code to fix the problem.
6.Stay tuned on this account for new updates.
